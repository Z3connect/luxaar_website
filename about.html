About Luxaar

Building a legacy of excellence, trust, and innovative construction solutions for over three decades.

Our Journey

Tracing our path from humble beginnings to a leader in premium construction.

1990 - The Foundation

Founded in 1990, Luxaar began as a small civil-engineering firm with a grand vision, initially focusing on local infrastructure projects and building a reputation for reliability.

2005 - Growth & Expansion

Expanded services to include commercial construction and comprehensive project management, taking on larger and more complex challenges.

2015 - Innovation & Sustainability

Achieved key industry certifications and fully embraced sustainable construction practices, integrating eco-friendly solutions into our projects.

Today - Leading the Way

With over 30+ years of experience, Luxaar is recognized as a leading premium construction solution provider, known for quality, innovation, and unwavering client commitment.

Our Vision

To redefine construction excellence through innovation, safety, and sustainable practices, becoming the most trusted name in the industry.

Our Mission

To deliver top-tier infrastructure projects on time, within budget, and with uncompromising quality, fostering lasting partnerships with our clients and communities.
